Our
Story

Nostalgia is a very complicated subject for me. I'm attracted by nostalgia but I refuse it intellectually. You can hide so much behind theatrics, and I don't need to do that any more. Awkwardness gives me great comfort. Every day I'm thinking about change. Fashion never stops. Sometimes the simplest things are the most profound.

Fashion is made to become unfashionable. I think there is something about luxury - it's not something people need, but it's what they want. It really pulls at their heart. It is important to be chic.
